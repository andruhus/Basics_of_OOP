{\bfseries{Google \mbox{\hyperlink{class_mock}{Mock}}}} is an extension to Google Test for writing and using C++ mock classes. See the separate \mbox{\hyperlink{md_lib_googletest-master_googlemock__r_e_a_d_m_e}{Google Mock documentation}}.
